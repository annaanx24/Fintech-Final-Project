What is Money?

Money is a form of exchange used to buy or sell things. If I give you a sandwich for a juice box, that's an exchange. In the real world, things can't be bought using sandwiches. Instead, you use coins (link), paper bills, and digital credit.

What is Credit and Debt?

If you borrow money from your parent or sibling, you need to pay it back! What if you have a deadline and miss it? You will have to pay even more! This is called credit, which comes from a lender (the person or place like a bank) and has interest if you don't pay back what you borrowed in time.
